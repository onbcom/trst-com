Frequently Asked Questions

What is digital trust infrastructure?

It is the foundational layer of protocols, cryptographic standards, and verification networks that allow digital entities (humans, organizations, and AI agents) to prove their identity, intent, and historical reliability in a secure, decentralized manner.

How does TRST verify AI agents?

Through a combination of cryptographic signatures tied to verified organizational wallets, runtime attestation environments, and behavioral heuristic checks that ensure the agent operates within strictly defined, auditable parameters.

What is decentralized identity (DID)?

DID is a framework that allows users to own, control, and share their identity data directly from personal secure enclaves (wallets) rather than relying on centralized corporate silos. It guarantees privacy while enabling seamless interoperability.

How is reputation calculated in 2026?

Reputation is dynamically calculated by aggregating historical transaction data, verifiable credentials, and dispute resolutions across federated trust networks. It uses zero-knowledge proofs to weigh reliability without exposing raw historical data.

Can I integrate the Trust Scorecard into my app?

Yes. TRST provides robust APIs and SDKs that allow platforms to embed our real-time Trust Scorecard, enabling automated access controls and risk-adjusted transaction routing within your existing architecture.
